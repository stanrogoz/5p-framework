Pause

Plan the objective, add context.

Before you prompt, get clear on what a good answer even looks like. Define the real decision, the audience, and the constraints — then load the AI with the context it needs.

  • State the decision and what “done” looks like.
  • Add the constraints, data, and audience.
  • Choose the format you actually need back.

Worked example — Picking a topic for a 15-minute meetup talk

The request:You've been offered a lightning-talk slot at a local meetup or town hall next month. What do you talk about?

The frame:Decision: which topic best serves the actual goal — and the goal needs naming: hiring visibility, plus personal credibility. Audience: ~50 people, mixed seniority, zero patience for a sales pitches. Done: a topic you can deliver well in 15 minutes that makes three people come talk to you afterwards.
