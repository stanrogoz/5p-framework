What the skill does

Once installed, the skill triggers whenever you ask your AI for a recommendation or a decision — "should we…", "which option is best…", or anything headed in front of stakeholders, a boss, or a client.

It walks the output through all five steps automatically:

  • Pause — frame the real decision, audience, and constraints.
  • Play — explore genuine alternatives before converging.
  • Push — stress-test assumptions and surface failure modes.
  • Polish — tighten it into a clear, shippable recommendation.
  • Present — anticipate objections and name the risks and trade-offs.
